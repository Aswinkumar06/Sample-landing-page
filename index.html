How We Work

From Husk to Export-Ready

Our 4-stage quality process ensures consistent, premium cocopeat every batch.

01
🥥
Raw Husk Sourcing
Fresh coconut husks sourced directly from partner farms across Kerala and Tamil Nadu, ensuring traceability and freshness.
02
🚿
Washing & Buffering
Multi-stage fresh water washing reduces EC and sodium content. Optional calcium-magnesium buffering available per order requirements.
03
🔬
Lab Testing & Grading
Every batch undergoes independent lab testing for EC, pH, moisture content, and pathogens before packaging.
04
📦
Packing & Export
Compressed and packed per client specs. Phytosanitary certified, fumigated pallets shipped from Chennai and Tuticorin ports.
