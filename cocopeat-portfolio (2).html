How We Work

From Husk to Export-Ready

Our 4-stage process ensures consistent, premium cocopeat every batch.

01
🥥
Raw Husk Sourcing
Fresh coconut husks sourced directly from partner farms across Kerala and Tamil Nadu with full traceability.
02
🚿
Washing & Buffering
Multi-stage freshwater washing reduces EC and sodium. Optional Ca/Mg buffering available per order.
03
🔬
Lab Testing & Grading
Every batch tested for EC, pH, moisture, and pathogens before packaging and dispatch.
04
📦
Packing & Export
Phytosanitary certified, fumigated pallets shipped from Chennai and Tuticorin ports worldwide.
